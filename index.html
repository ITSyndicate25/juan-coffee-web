Our Story

Wayback 2020, the Juan Koppee opened in Calamba, Laguna. It is a social venture promoting ethically source, farmer-first, direct trade coffee from Philippine Indigenous growers. The Juan Koppee brand is high standards make our coffee uniquely and uniformly delicious no matter which location you visit, anywhere in the world. You can enjoy our renowned coffees at home, school, the office or wherever travels take you.
